FAQ

What areas do you service?

Kansas City and surrounding areas within about an hour.

Do you use Chemicals?

Yes! But dont be afraid! We have a specialized cleaning process that keeps all your furniture, plants, and all surounding vegetation SAFE from the industry standard chemical that will be used.

How much do you charge?

We charge by the square footage of surfaces being cleaned.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes. We offer Fast quotes for residential and commercial jobs through an online quoting process. We just need basic information like your home or business address to get started!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept most major forms of payment including credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments. At this time we do not accept checks.

What types of surfaces can you clean?

We professionally clean a variety of exterior surfaces including: • Driveways and concrete • House siding • Roofs • Gutters • Patios and sidewalks • Fences • Commercial building exteriors If you’re unsure whether we can clean something, just ask!

How long does pressure washing take?

Most residential projects such as driveways, patios, or house washing can be completed within a few hours. Larger homes or commercial jobs may take longer depending on the size and condition of the surfaces being cleaned.

Do I need to be home during the service?

In most cases you do not need to be home as long as we have access to the areas being cleaned and an outdoor water source if required. We will communicate with you before and after the service to ensure everything is completed properly.

Will pressure washing damage my property?

When done correctly using professional equipment and proper techniques, pressure washing is safe and highly effective. We adjust pressure levels and cleaning solutions depending on the surface to protect your property while achieving great results.
